Privacy Policy

Math Genius

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Welcome to Math Genius. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Math Genius is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app. Any data generated during gameplay is stored locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We collect minimal technical information necessary for the app to function properly:

  • Game Progress Data: Your scores, difficulty levels, and game statistics are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve user experience
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as operating system version to ensure compatibility

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Financial information

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and statistics locally on your device
  • Provide you with a personalized gaming experience
  • Track your performance and improvement over time
  • Maintain app functionality and stability
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your game data, including scores, progress, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored using industry-standard encryption methods
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers
  • Regular security updates to address potential vulnerabilities

4. Third-Party Services

Math Genius does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your information with third parties because we don't collect personal information in the first place.

The app may use the following platform services:

  • Expo Framework: Used for app development and deployment
  • React Native: The framework powering the app's functionality

These services do not collect personal information from our app.

5. Children's Privacy

Math Genius is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • The app does not require registration or account creation
  • No in-app purchases or advertisements that target children
  • Parents can safely allow their children to use the app without privacy concerns

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app's statistics and settings
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and can be backed up using your device's backup features

6.2 Opting Out

Since we don't collect personal information or use tracking services, there are no opt-out procedures necessary. Simply using the app as intended ensures your privacy.

7. Data Retention

Your game data is retained on your device for as long as you have the app installed. When you uninstall Math Genius, all associated data is automatically removed from your device.

8. International Users

Math Genius can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't transfer data internationally, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app or app store updates
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

10. Permissions

Math Genius requests minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your game progress and statistics locally
  • Internet (if applicable): Only for app updates, not for data collection

We do not request access to sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location.
